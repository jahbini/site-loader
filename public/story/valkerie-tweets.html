Per a reader's request for a story for diary entries about a Valkyrie.  I changed the "diary" idea into using tweets instead; more of a challenge.

I got some background at this site -- http://lokis-dottir.livejournal.com/68127.html

I thought it might be fun to make Aubrun a low-ranking wage slave in some corporate heirarchy.

The following is intended to be a real-time twitter performance between two myth-tweets (mythical tweeters) -- hey, who says spiritual beings cant be on the internet.

Aubrun: - Valkerie  -- Addresses her tweets to @Skuld

Skuld - Senior Valkerie  -- replies back to @Aubrun

Sessrumnir: -- Valhalla's waiting station, A giant hall of limbo with wooden benches for the dead.

Skuld: @Aubrun assignment: 11/23/09 08:30 UTC You have been re-assigned to Darfur sector 3B. report immediately

Aubrun: @Skuld K

Skuld: @Aubrun upon arrival you will choose souls from only the dead, not like in your last assignment.

Aubrun: @Skuld but he was in a coma!  That’s as good as dead!

Skuld: @Aubrun no, we went all over that during the inquiry.  I don’t know why Odin stepped in and gave you another chance, but wise up.

Aubrun: @Skuld OK, I’ve learned my lesson.  I’m not to CHOOSE any but really dead souls from the battles.

Skuld: @Aubrun That’s right, returns are 2 expensive in temporal and spiritual corrections that must be made.

Skuld: @Aubrun Begin your reports from Darfur sector 3B immediately.

Sessrumnir: @Skuld 11/23/09 08:45 UTC 1EA soul labeled 35273 deposited to Sessrumnir input

Sessrumnir: @Skuld 11/23/09 08:51 UTC 1EA soul labeled 35985 deposited to Sessrumnir input

Sessrumnir: @Skuld 11/23/09 08:52 UTC 1EA soul labeled 37226 deposited to Sessrumnir input

Aubrun: Sessrumnir numbers are going up fast! from 35273 to 37226 in 7 minutes! Too many dead today! 

Skuld: @Aubrun You know, I read that too, and you get paid. Just be happy you are still working

Aubrun: @Skuld K, but it’s lots of work and long hours down here as a Valkyrie.

Skuld: @Aubrun You get maximum pay for your job specification.  If you need, go to HR.

Aubrun: @Skuld No, I’m not going to HR again: all smiles and coo-ing.  Liars all.

Sessrumnir: @Skuld 11/23/09 09:11 UTC 1EA soul labeled 38177 deposited to Sessrumnir input

Aubrun: @Skuld I'm at Sessrumnir processing says they have 4 souls destined for Valhalla, and want me to take them

Skuld: @Aubrun Nix on the escort to Vahallah, you don’t have seniority, and you are only qualified for 2 souls transport.

Aubrun: @Skuld  Sorry, I already took all 4.  I’m in transit to Valhalla, I’ll check in there.

Skuld: @Aubrun Valhalla reports that you did not arrive as scheduled.  U there?

Skuld: @Aubrun U there?  REPORT!

Skuld: @Aubrun  Where are you?  What have you done with those souls??

Skuld: @Aubrun Odin won’t save you this time, you better check in NOW.

Aubrun: @Skuld Um.  K. I’m having a little trouble.  Still en-route.

Skuld: @Aubrun  Don’t tell me you dropped a soul again!  And this one was for Valhalla?  Odin will be pissed.

Aubrun: @Skuld  No sweat, just tell him that Loki tripped me up again.

Skuld: @Aubrun LOL!  I used that excuse myself when I was still doing soul-transport. Always Works.

Aubrun: @Skuld yes Loki ALWAYS admits to everything just to piss ODIN off! :-)
